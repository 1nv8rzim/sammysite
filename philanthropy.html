Who We Support

Every year we hold a number of philanthropy events to support several organizations. The main organizations we work with and raise money for are The Judy Fund, The Make-A-Wish Foundation, and American Foundation for Suicide Prevention.

Polesit

This event is one our chapter has put on since receiving our charter and is the largest event we organize every year. PoleSit consists of a brother sitting in our "Crow's Nest" at the top of a 30 ft telephone pole at all times for an entire week. Throughout the week, the chapter has multiple events at the base of the pole for the RIT community to take advantage of and learn more about the Make-A-Wish Foundation.

Pie A Sammy

This event is put on to support The Judy Fund and is usually held on or around "Pi Day" (March 14). We hang out in a quad all day and anyone can pay just $1 to pie any brother in the face.

ActNow

Sigma Alpha Mu Fraternity started working with the RIT Wellness Department to present ActNow, a Suicide Awareness and Prevention Program, in 2017. Our Rochester Regional Health sponsored symposium event consisted of speakers from the RIT and local community, sharing their own stories as well as resources on how everyone can be a part of suicide prevention. To learn more how you can be involved please visit www.actnowroc.com and learn more about our future and past events. Together we can put an end to this pressing issues in our society.
